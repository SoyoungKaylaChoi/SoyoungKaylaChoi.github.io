Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in College of Nursing at the Pennsylvania State University with resaerch foci on the healthcare for heart failure patients and their family caregivers. In addition, my further research will focus on the utilization of digital healthcare technologies for managing the chronic illness of people with visual impairments. I love to learn data science skills and employ an innovative approach to my research; To employ text mining techniques to my dissertation work, I took a computer scienece course at Penn State. I believe my approach toward the quantification of textual data will contribute to the development of novel methods for generating fascinating research questions in nursing science.
