Three Available Packages Specialized for Visually Impaired R Users

There are three packages for the blind R users (Godfrey, Warren, Murrell, Bilton, & Sorge, 2018; Seo, 2017; Siegert & Williams, 2017).

Methods

Creswell (2014) suggests a mixed methods study as a balanced methodology between qualitative and quantitative research.
